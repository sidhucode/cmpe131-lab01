Branches enable for teams to test various changes and features freely without interrupting or causing conflict with other team member efforts. It offers a solution such that differences can be viewed and adjusted before pushing to the master branch. It can prevent cases where a crucial feature that was being worked on by one developer get's removed, deleted, or crashes with errors as a result of changes, or new libraries that are introduced by another developer.
